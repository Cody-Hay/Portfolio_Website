Free Zombie Game

Free Zombie Game is a top-down tower defence style game where you have to defend scrap from zombies with traps you can upgrade or create better traps with the scrap. I worked on a few of the traps, such as the Fire Turret, and helped out on the Sand pit, but the majority of my work on this game was the VFX, which I made most, if not all, of them.
